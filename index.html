Design Models

Instructional Design

I approach each project with a strong human experiential process. I have a strong foundation of training theories (.e.g. ADDIE, storyboarding...) and principles of adult learning theory. I have knowledge and experience with developing educational materials and learning management systems.

User Interaction Design

How a user will interact with content can have a great impact on a learning experience. With a keenness and passion to engage learner's desire for stimulation, I use UI models and frameworks to assist, solve, and analyze exceptional intuitive interactions.

Learning Experience Design

The experience that a learner has while learning, such as the at-home learning environment, is taken into the overall design of all material and content that I design. Content improved upon by a feedback loop between learners through diagnosis and discovery to development and delivery.
